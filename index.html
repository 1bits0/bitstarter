While life may be a game of repetition, from time to time there comes moments never experienced before in your life. Some of these events may be so astonishing. Some may happen in an ordinary day and brighten it. Some of them took you by surprise, yet some may be scheduled by you. In all cases you may want to share your experience and if so, here is an opportunity to do so.

Why use Firstimz?

  • you can reach more people with your story than if it's tucked in a general purpose social networks or blog.
  • You can also easily search/browse other first time experiences all in one place.
  • You can use your existing twitter, facebook or google+ logins to get to it.

FAQ

What is Firstimz?
Firstimz is a social networking program for people to share their first time experience of anything in life.
Do I need to have an account with Firsttimz to post my exciting first time experience of anything?
If you already have accounts with twitter, facebook or google+, you can signin using them. Otherwise, creating a new account is as simple as providing a valid email and a new password.
What is the privacy setup I need to complete?
There is no complicated privacy rules to setup in Firstimz. While your account information (email and password) remain private to you alone, the stories you share remain available to the general public. You have the option of removing your stories from Firstimz permanently.
In what ways can I share my experiences?
You can express it in a text of upto 500 characters, upload a picture, audio or video or a combination of all.
